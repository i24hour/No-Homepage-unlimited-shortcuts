iCTrL is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your data stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

We do NOT collect any personal information. iCTrL operates entirely locally on your browser. Here's what the extension stores locally on your device:

  • Quick Links: The shortcut links you create (names and URLs)
  • RAM Saver Settings: Your preferences for auto-close timeout and protected domains
  • Tab Activity Data: Timestamps of when tabs were last active (for RAM Saver feature only)
Important

All data is stored using Chrome's local storage API (chrome.storage.local) and never leaves your device. We have no servers, no databases, and no way to access your data.

2. Permissions Explained

iCTrL requires the following browser permissions to function:

Permission Why We Need It
storage To save your quick links, settings, and preferences locally on your device
tabs To track tab activity for the RAM Saver feature and open new tabs when you click shortcuts
alarms To periodically check for inactive tabs (RAM Saver runs every minute in background)

3. Data Sharing

We do NOT share any data with third parties.

  • No analytics or tracking services
  • No advertising networks
  • No data brokers
  • No external APIs (except Google Favicon API for displaying website icons)

4. Data Security

Your data is protected by Chrome's built-in security mechanisms:

  • All data is stored in Chrome's sandboxed storage
  • Only this extension can access its own stored data
  • Data is tied to your Chrome profile and synced only if you enable Chrome Sync

5. Your Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • View: Open the extension popup to see all your saved shortcuts and settings
  • Edit: Modify or delete any shortcut at any time
  • Delete All: Uninstalling the extension will remove all stored data
  • Export: Your Chrome profile backup includes extension data

6. Children's Privacy

iCTrL does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The extension does not collect any personal information from any user.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
