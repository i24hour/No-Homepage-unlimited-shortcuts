## 1. Information We Collect

**We do NOT collect any personal information.**

iCTrL operates entirely locally on your browser. Here's what the extension stores locally on your device:

| Data Type | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| Quick Links | The shortcut links you create (names and URLs) |
| RAM Saver Settings | Your preferences for auto-close timeout and protected domains |
| Tab Activity Data | Timestamps of when tabs were last active (for RAM Saver feature only) |

> **Important:** All data is stored using Chrome's local storage API (`chrome.storage.local`) and never leaves your device. We have no servers, no databases, and no way to access your data.

---

## 2. Permissions Explained

iCTrL requires the following browser permissions to function:

| Permission | Why We Need It |
|------------|----------------|
| `storage` | To save your quick links, settings, and preferences locally on your device |
| `tabs` | To track tab activity for the RAM Saver feature and open new tabs when you click shortcuts |
| `alarms` | To periodically check for inactive tabs (RAM Saver runs every minute in background) |
